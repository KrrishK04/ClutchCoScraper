BACKGROUND

Introduce your business and what you do there.

I’m the head of delivery at Memrise, an online language learning platform. My role is to ensure that the product, engineering, and customer service departments are organized and that we have predictable feature delivery. I also ensure that our day-to-day operations function smoothly.

OPPORTUNITY / CHALLENGE

What challenge were you trying to address with Vention?

We needed resources to augment our team.

SOLUTION

What was the scope of their involvement?

Vention provides us with staff augmentation services. The team manages our mobile, web, and backend platforms. For our mobile team, we've split it into Android and iOS.

We've ensured each platform has two people because people go on holiday and need breaks. We've made a conscious effort to have a partner for our web, iOS, and Android platforms.

For our backend team, we leverage DevOps and backend engineers who build our capabilities. They're a much larger group of around six teammates, so we've scaled that team further.

What is the team composition?

We primarily contact Jovi and Andrei. Jovi has been our account manager, and Andrei has also been involved with our project. We employ around six full-time teammates from Vention, including mobile, front- and backend engineers, UX designers, and QA engineers.

How did you come to work with Vention?

Our CPO introduced us to Vention. He had previously worked with them and had such a good experience. Since we were going through the COVID-19 pandemic, we needed remote resources, so we gave them a trial, and we've been happy ever since.

How much have you invested with them?

We've spent over £200,000.

What is the status of this engagement?

We started working together in June 2020, and the engagement is ongoing.

RESULTS & FEEDBACK

What evidence can you share that demonstrates the impact of the engagement?

Vention has provided us with a very good caliber of talent. They have excellent communication skills and are very good collaborators. They have a great mindset of getting things done. We've integrated them well with our UK permanent staff.

For example, we've had an old and untouched architecture for our iOS team, and it's been tough to build things on it. We've ended up changing five files, which requires lots of testing. However, we can deploy something in half an hour, making us more operationally efficient and helping with costs.

How did Vention perform from a project management standpoint?

We use Jira, Trello, and GitHub to manage our projects and leverage Asana for design prototyping. The team uses LISA for user research. We communicate via Google Meet, Zoom, and Slack.

What did you find most impressive about them?

Vention cares about their employees. They want to ensure their team is treated well and make every effort to ensure we have the proper structure. We regularly meet with our account manager to understand what's happening from our perspective and vice versa. We get lots of feedback, which is crucial for us.

When we have a brief, Vention constantly excels at understanding our needs and asking questions. They're efficient and meet our needs, which is very valuable. We don't feel they want to hire anyone to get a fee out of it. They're very personable, and we have a good relationship.

Are there any areas they could improve?

No, there aren't any.

Do you have any advice for potential customers?

Vention is very personable, so it's helpful to be clear on your brief. Talk to the team about how you want to work because everybody works differently. Have an initial conversation where you share how you want to interact with Vention so they can create the right plan.
